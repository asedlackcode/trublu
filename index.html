Get to know us

We are a California Licensed C-10 Holder, License No. 1021344. With over 35 years experience as an electrician, licensed in 2016, we specialize in Sprung Structure wiring. Our most recent projects include the Riverside Bridge Home, in Los Angeles, Beacon Bridge Home, in San Pedro and Eubank Bridge Home in Wilmington, CA.

No job is too big or too small. We also do homeowner improvements including rewires, panel upgrades, ceiling fan installation, yard lighting and much more. Our major clients include Auto Club, First American Insurance and the City of Los Angeles. Our prices are reasonable and we are dedicated to providing the best service possible to our clients! We are of course licensed, bonded and insured and guarantee our client's satisfaction in all of our projects.
